Our Story

With over 40 years of experience serving the Seattle area, we’ve dedicated ourselves to helping families create homes they cherish. If it’s adding those meaningful personal touches or undertaking significant renovations, we commit to being by your side throughout the entire journey.

When you choose Manos Construction, you’re partnering with a team that genuinely invests in your vision. We understand that a home is the backdrop for your life’s most important moments, and we are honored to play a role in bringing that vision to life.

Our approach is straightforward: we listen to your needs, provide honest insights, and deliver exceptional results. This philosophy has fostered lasting relationships with clients who return to us time and again, knowing they can rely on our dedication and professionalism.

OUR WORK PROCESS

01

Let’s Chat

We start with a consultation to understand your vision and style. It’s an open dialogue where you can share your ideas, allowing us to capture your preferences.

02

Design & Plan

Based on your vision, we create detailed designs, selecting materials and finishes that match your style.You’ll be involved throughout, with regular updates.

03

Bring It to Life

With the plan in place, our team manages the construction, handling everything from start to finish, keeping things efficient and on track.

04

Celebrate & Enjoy

Once complete, we do a final walkthrough to ensure everything’s perfect. Then, it’s time for you to enjoy your beautifully reimagined space.
