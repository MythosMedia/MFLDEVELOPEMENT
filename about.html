About Us

Expert Local Builders

MFL Developments is Professional, Local, and Trusted in BC.

Based in Richmond, MFL Developments provides general contracting and project management services in the scope of luxury residential home building across the lower mainland.

We provide exclusivity, luxury, and cutting edge design for our clients by engaging only the most skilled craftsmen and procuring the finest premium materials for the project.

Let’s Chat
